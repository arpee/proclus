Saito is a big-data blockchain for distributed web applications. We achieve the scalability to do this by combining a transient blockchain with a proof-of-transactions that pays nodes for transmitting messages.

We have a few applications under development, but there is much work to do and we are a small team. If you are interested in helping us decentralize the social-web and protect free-speech online, please get in touch.
