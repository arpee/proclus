Saito is a big-data blockchain for distributed web applications. We achieve the scalability to do this by combining a transient blockchain with a proof-of-transactions approach that pays nodes for contributing bandwidth to the network.

If you are interested in helping us decentralize the social-web and protect freedom of speech online, please get in touch.
